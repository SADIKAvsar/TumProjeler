## Strategic Analysis Required

Waiting on the map is beneficial when:
✅ Next boss spawns soon (<90 seconds)
✅ Both bosses are on same map (no travel needed)
✅ Historical success rate for this wait is >70%
✅ Time saved > time lost from staying idle

Returning to farm is better when:
❌ Next boss spawns far in future (>120 seconds)
❌ Bosses are on different maps (travel anyway)
❌ Risk of death/disconnect while waiting
❌ Better to farm exp than idle

## Mathematical Optimization

**Time Cost of Waiting**: {time_until_next} seconds idle
**Time Cost of Returning**: ~{return_time} seconds travel + waiting

**Break-even point**: If `time_until_next < (return_time + {travel_time})`, waiting is optimal.

## Risk Factors

Consider:
- Boss might not spawn exactly on time (player interference, server lag)
- Waiting in combat zone = risk of PvP death
- Network disconnection during wait = position lost
- Resource consumption (HP/MP) during idle
